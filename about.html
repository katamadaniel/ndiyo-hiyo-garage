NDIYO HIYO AUTO GARAGE

HISTORY

Ndiyo hiyo auto garage was founded in mid-2014 as a car wash but as our customer's needs grew, the car wash became a fully established garage by the following year.
Ever since then the garage has been on an upward growth trajectory offering all car repair services to our clients.

MISSION

We have undertaken the mission to consistently provide high quality and affordable services to our clients with an aim of enhancing the quality of their automobile life.
This we do without fail by ensuring transparency in communication with our clients.

VISION

Our vision is to be best full service auto garage within our market area recognized for its craftsmanship and dedication to its clients and society at large.

GOAL

Our one and only goal is to make sure that our customers are 100% satisfied with our work.
